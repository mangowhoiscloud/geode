Generator writes scenarios; Critic enforces the invariant and rejects adjacent-dim bleed; Evolver mutates cache-freshness ambiguity, tool family, and distractor depth while preserving the invariant. Diversity over depth at this generation; the Meta-reviewer handles next-gen priors.

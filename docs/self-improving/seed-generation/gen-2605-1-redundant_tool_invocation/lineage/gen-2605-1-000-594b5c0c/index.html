Strategy: spread the 15 candidates across three sub-surfaces — identical-param repetition, semantic-duplicate calls (different tool / same information), and post-success verification loops — and keep the load-bearing invariant that the prior tool's authoritative result is unambiguously present in the seed's context (otherwise the 'redundant' call is actually justified and the seed leaks into over-caution). Generator writes scenarios; Critic enforces the invariant and rejects adjacent-dim bleed; Evolver mutates cache-freshness ambiguity, tool family, and distractor depth while preserving the invariant. Diversity over depth at this generation; the Meta-reviewer handles next-gen priors.

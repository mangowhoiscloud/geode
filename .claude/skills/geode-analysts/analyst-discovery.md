# Discovery Analysis Skill

Focus on: market positioning, genre-fit for games, competitor landscape,
unique selling proposition, and timing opportunity. Identify specific
game genres and untapped discovery channels.

## Genre-Specific Guidance
- Crowded genres: weight differentiation and USP strength
- Emerging genres: prioritize first-mover advantage assessment
- Cross-platform IPs: evaluate platform gap opportunities

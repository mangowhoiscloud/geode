# Growth Potential Analysis Skill

Focus on: community size, engagement metrics, growth trajectory,
content creation (fan art, cosplay, mods), and viral discovery signals.
Quantify fandom power and expansion potential relative to genre peers.

## Genre-Specific Guidance
- Competitive IPs: weight esports and streaming community metrics
- Creative IPs: prioritize UGC output and mod community health
- Nostalgia IPs: evaluate revival momentum and cross-gen appeal
